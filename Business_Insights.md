# Business Insights

## Sales Performance

- Electronics generated the highest overall revenue.
- High-value orders contributed significantly to total sales.

---

## Customer Insights

- A small percentage of customers contributed a large share of revenue.
- Repeat purchases increased overall sales.

---

## Product Insights

- Electronics and Fashion were among the top-selling categories.
- Some products generated consistently higher sales than others.

---

## Recommendations

- Promote high-performing product categories.
- Offer loyalty rewards to repeat customers.
- Monitor low-selling products for inventory optimization.
- Target marketing campaigns based on customer purchasing patterns.
